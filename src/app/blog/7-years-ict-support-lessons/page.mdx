# What 7 Years in ICT Support Taught Me About Business Growth

From Dry Associates to Oracle Edge, ICT support isn’t only fixing—it's enabling. The best support teams drive reliability, speed, and confidence.

- Automate the boring: scripts, templates, and documentation save hours.
- Communicate early: stakeholders care about status and impact more than logs.
- Measure outcomes: uptime, MTTR, and user satisfaction drive budgets.
